Privacy policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

Who we are

Gotta Minute Studios operates mobile games including Shadow Minute. We are based in the United Kingdom. Contact: gottaminutestudios@gmail.com

Data we collect

We do not collect personal data directly. Our apps use Google AdMob to serve advertisements, which may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Local storage

Game progress and high scores are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-party services

We use Google AdMob for advertising. Please refer to Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy for information on how they handle data.

Children's privacy

Our games are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold. As we collect no personal data directly, there is nothing to access or delete on our end. For AdMob data, contact Google directly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
